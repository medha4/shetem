Our Purpose

SheTEM is a non-profit organization dedicated towards bringing STEM to girls all over the world. SheTEM is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization which is working towards exposing underprivileged girls to STEM. SheTEM believes that SheCan and holds workshops, seminars, and posts videos online to inspire impoverished youth to pursue careers in STEM.
